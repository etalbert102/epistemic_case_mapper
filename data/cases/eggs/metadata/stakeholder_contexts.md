# Eggs Stakeholder Contexts

Purpose: make the case useful for realistic reasoning contexts.

## Contexts

### Healthy Adult

Question: Can eggs be part of a generally healthy diet?

Relevant structure:

- dietary pattern,
- substitution context,
- dose,
- overall CVD risk.

### Person With Diabetes Or Prediabetes

Question: Does the egg evidence differ for diabetes-related risk?

Relevant structure:

- subgroup findings,
- glycemic and CVD endpoints,
- baseline cardiometabolic risk,
- guideline caution.

### Person With High LDL-C

Question: Should egg intake be limited because of lipid response?

Relevant structure:

- RCT lipid-marker findings,
- LDL-C/HDL-C ratio,
- individual cholesterol response,
- clinical outcome uncertainty.

### Public-Health Guideline Writer

Question: What general advice is robust despite uncertainty?

Relevant structure:

- population-level risk management,
- food pattern rather than isolated foods,
- feasibility of simple guidance,
- communication risk.

### Evidence Reviewer

Question: Which evidence should be trusted for which inference?

Relevant structure:

- cohort confounding,
- meta-analysis overlap,
- surrogate vs clinical outcomes,
- population heterogeneity.
