# LHC Stakeholder Contexts

Purpose: make the LHC case useful for realistic reasoning contexts.

## Contexts

### Particle Physicist

Question: Are the physical assumptions and astrophysical constraints coherent?

Relevant structure:

- microscopic black-hole production assumptions,
- Hawking radiation,
- stable-object scenarios,
- cosmic-ray and astronomical-body constraints.

### Institutional Reviewer

Question: Is the safety assessment independently reviewed and decision-ready?

Relevant structure:

- LSAG report,
- SPC review,
- scope caveats,
- technical vs institutional authority.

### Public-Risk Critic

Question: Were low-probability catastrophic scenarios dismissed too quickly?

Relevant structure:

- Plaga critique,
- response assumptions,
- legal/public-risk framing,
- burden of proof.

### Judge Or Legal Decision-Maker

Question: How should courts handle speculative catastrophic risk with technical disagreement?

Relevant structure:

- Johnson legal framing,
- epistemic competence,
- injunction standards,
- institutional deference.

### Science Communicator

Question: How can the safety case be explained without flattening it into mere reassurance?

Relevant structure:

- public CERN pages,
- distinction between "no danger" and "here are the dependencies,"
- caveats and model scope.
