# LHC Source Independence Notes

Purpose: prevent institutional summaries and technical analyses from being counted as independent when they share arguments.

## Independence Clusters

### Formal Safety / Review Cluster

- `lsag_2008_safety_review`
- `spc_2008_lsag_review`
- `giddings_mangano_2008_stable_black_holes`

Risk: SPC review and LSAG review are distinct documents, but the SPC review evaluates LSAG and Giddings-Mangano rather than providing an entirely independent physical argument.

### Public CERN Communication Cluster

- `cern_lhc_current_page`
- `cern_tiny_black_holes_page`

Risk: useful for public-facing explanation, but not independent technical evidence.

### Empirical Search Cluster

- `cms_2011_black_hole_search`
- `cms_2010_black_hole_search_paper`

Risk: public summary and technical paper describe closely related empirical searches. Count as one evidence cluster unless distinguishing public communication vs technical detail.

### Critique / Response Cluster

- `plaga_2008_metastable_black_holes`
- `giddings_mangano_2008_comments_plaga`

Risk: important adversarial exchange, but the response is not independent of the original Giddings-Mangano technical position.

### Governance / Legal Cluster

- `johnson_2009_black_hole_case`

Risk: useful for reasoning about decision procedure, not direct physics evidence.

## Cruxes For Worked Region

- Does the natural cosmic-ray argument cover collider-specific conditions?
- Which assumptions about microscopic-black-hole stability and accretion are necessary?
- Does the astrophysical survival argument depend on white dwarfs/neutron stars in ways a lay summary might omit?
- What did the public controversy reveal about decision-making under catastrophic uncertainty?
