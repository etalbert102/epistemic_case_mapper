## Goal

Produce a judge-facing LHC black hole risk source-grounded draft demo that can be regenerated from the repo and inspected without relying on chat history.

Source acquisition has been executed. The remaining work is to audit and refine source-grounded claims, relations, report structure, and validation output. The artifact must remain `draft` or `human-review-needed` until human review occurs.

## Non-Goals

- Do not attempt the COVID origins case.
- Do not build a product UI.
- Do not claim source-grounded status while using only seed notes.
- Do not add unrelated refactors.
- Do not mark the output human-reviewed.

## Evidence Mode

Initial target: source-grounded draft.

Source corpus: `data/cases/lhc_black_holes/sources/`.

Web use: not required for this plan unless the user explicitly authorizes additional source acquisition.
